Traditional Ragu Bolognese

Description

Super delicious, easy, and cheap bolognese recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1kg Fatty Beef Mince
  • Passata
  • 3-5 Bay Leaves
  • Can of whole tomatoes
  • 2 medium-large onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 2-3 cups of wine of your choice
  • Pasta of your choice (penne and tagiatelle work well, spaghetti is undesirable)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Saute onion, carrot and celery in a generous amount of olive oil at least until onion is transparent. (small burnt bits add flavour)
  2. Add mince and cook until browned and excess water is cooked out. (again burnt bits add flavour)
  3. Add wine and cook off alcohol.
  4. Add passata, whole tomatoes (crush them with woode spoon), bay leaves, and give initial seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Fill empty Passata glass with water and add that to allow for evaporation.
  6. Cook for at least 2-3 hours
  7. Do a final seasoning to fine tune to your taste
  8. Cook your pasta to packet directions
  9. Add pasta and sauce to saucepan and cook together for a few minutes to combine.
  10. Enjoy!
